001 about

I'm a sophomore at Oregon State University – Cascades studying Engineering Science. Most of my work stems from personal passion — analog synthesizers, small photography tools, and custom climbing and mountaineering gear. Outside of school I find solace in the mountains — climbing, mountaineering, skiing, and paragliding. I'm drawn to difficult things. When I'm not outside I'm usually making something, whether that's music, photos, videos, or whatever project I've engrossed myself in.

analog modular synthesizer

A fully analog modular synthesizer capable of generating and shaping audio signals through voltage-controlled modules. The system integrates a VCO, VCF, VCA, and Slew Limiter to produce a wide range of novel synthesized sounds. Designed, prototyped, and debugged entirely by hand.

analog circuit design / spice simulation / soldering / oscilloscope / schematic reading / breadboard prototyping / vco, vcf, vca / signal analysis

02 engr 103

hand crank generator

A hand-powered electrical generator designed and built from scratch. The gear mechanism was modeled in Fusion 360 and 3D printed, then integrated with a microcontroller to regulate and measure power output.

python / fusion 360 / 3d printing / mechanical design / wiring and assembly / data logging / power analysis / microcontroller
